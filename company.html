About us

Our Company Journey

From a small dream to a thriving jewelry brand, discover how Bling's Accessories has evolved over the years to become your favorite destination for stylish and affordable accessories.

2020

The Beginning

Bling's Accessories was founded by Jhester Pascual in his small apartment. With just 50 handmade pieces, he launched an online store that quickly gained attention for its unique designs.

2021

First Expansion

After overwhelming response from customers, we moved to our first small studio and expanded our team to five passionate designers and craftspeople.

2022

Brand Recognition

Bling's Accessories was featured in several fashion magazines and won the "Rising Star" award in the accessories category at the National Fashion Awards.

2023

Global Reach

We expanded our shipping to international markets and established partnerships with sustainable material suppliers to enhance our product quality while reducing environmental impact.

2024

New Headquarters

To accommodate our growing team of 25 employees, we moved to our current headquarters featuring a state-of-the-art design studio and fulfillment center.

2025

Present & Future

Today, Bling's Accessories serves over 100,000 happy customers worldwide. We're constantly innovating and looking forward to bringing you even more stunning accessories in the years to come.

Our Core Values

Quality & Craft

We believe that accessories should be made to last. Every piece is inspected to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Customer Happiness

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We're committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring every Bling's customer feels valued and heard.

Sustainability

We're dedicated to reducing our environmental impact by using eco-friendly packaging and ethically sourced materials whenever possible.
